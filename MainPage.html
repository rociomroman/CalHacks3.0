About Us

Digno is dedicated to the pursuit of political engagement within the community. We want to give everyone a chance to let their voice be heard! To help foster political discourse, Make A Change empowers visitors to join with their community, either in protest or in discussion. After the results of the most recent election, the founders of Digno realized that the lack of direct political communication between members of the electorate, even between members of the same party, was flawed and detrimental to our democracy.
